CO-FOUNDER AND COO

Paul Starrett, EnCE, CFE, is an attorney and private investigator specializing in privacy and data-protection technology. He specializes in automated integration, artificial intelligence and cybersecurity-risk consulting. His 25-year career includes roles as general counsel, chief risk officer and as an information-security software engineer. Paul holds a Master’s degree in Predictive Analytics from Northwestern University and a Master of Laws (LL.M.) in Taxation from Golden Gate University. Paul is the original founder of PrivacyLabs and is its Chief Operating Officer and Majority Shareholder. Paul is exceptionally proud of his recent appointment to Vice-president of Janitorial Services of PrivacyLabs.

TECHNOLOGY AND AI COUNSEL

Stephen Wu is an attorney and shareholder with Silicon Valley Law Group in San Jose, CA. He advises clients on privacy, security, transactions, compliance, liability, and governance of information technologies, such as artificial intelligence, autonomous vehicles, robotics, Big Data, and the Internet of Things. He negotiates technology agreements, resolves disputes, and serves as an outside general counsel for emerging companies. An author of seven security legal books and numerous other publications, Steve is the Chair of the American Bar Association Artificial Intelligence & Robotics National Institute. Steve was the 2010-11 Chair of the American Bar Association Science & Technology Law Section. He graduated from Harvard Law School in 1988 and was VeriSign’s second in-house attorney.
